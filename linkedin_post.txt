🚀 Excited to share the completion of my second task for the SkillCraft Technology Data Science Internship! (Task SCT_DS_2) 📊

For this project, I dove into the famous Kaggle Titanic dataset to perform Data Cleaning and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA). Here's a brief look at the workflow:
✨ Cleaned the data by handling missing values (imputing median Age and mode Embarked).
✨ Dropped highly sparse or complex categorical features (Cabin, Ticket, Name) to focus on a clean baseline.
✨ Built a comprehensive 2x2 visual dashboard using Python, Pandas, Seaborn, and Matplotlib.

Key Insights Discovered:
🔹 Survival rates were significantly higher for female passengers.
🔹 First-class passengers had a distinctly better survival outcome compared to other classes.
🔹 Age distribution analysis showed that younger demographic segments (like children) had a higher proportion of survival.

Check out the generated dashboard attached to this post! It's been a great exercise in converting raw tabular data into meaningful, actionable visual insights.
